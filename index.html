About Us

The Project

Project Reach Out is a humble beginning towards creating a community of helping hands and listening ears. It often happens that you feel lost and alone going through life. However, you are never really alone as someone is out there who has gone through what you are going through. It just needs a little sharing to find out that person. We do not want anyone to feel alone. Project Reach Out gives you a platform to share your ups and downs in an anonymous manner and let people reach out to you. Also, it helps you in reaching out to a complete stranger who is in distress and feeling a little lonely.
